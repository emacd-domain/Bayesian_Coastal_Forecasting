Contained herein are 9 directories following 9 objectives for the complete preprocessing, build, run and evaluation of both Bayesian Forecasting algorithms.

O1_location_selection
Establishing and computational domain

O2_era5_variables
Extracting drivers from era5 variable files at domain locations

O3_pca
Application of principal component analysis

O4_millport_surge
Arrangement of Millport surge timeseries

O5_designmatrix
Designmatrix construction and normalisation

O6_lstm_train
Surge forecast model training

O7_abms_run
ABMS algorithm validation 

O8_abmc_visual_crossing_forecast_variables
Extracting drivers from visual crossing weather forecasts and calculating uncertainties with respect to the training data

O9_mcba_run
MCBA algorithm validation
