Contained herein are the functions for Monte Carlo Bayesian Average and Adaptive Bayesian Model Selection Forecasting.

Examples of their usage are shown through the surge and wind-wave applications in the adjacent directories.

plots_and_metrics.py
takes the output of a set of robust predictions and produces graphics and metrics for analysis

load_save_functions.py
reads in models for use in model selection algorithm

model_selection_algorithms.py
applies either the ABMS or MCBA algorithms to the input data

posterior_evaluation_functions.py
functions for calculating the posterior probabilities and fitting Gaussian Mixture Models
